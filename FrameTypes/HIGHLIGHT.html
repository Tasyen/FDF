FrameType HIGHLIGHT

Displays Textures (dds, blp, tga, tif) for the Frame Family. They have various ways to interpret/use the given textures alpha.

In Warcraft 3 V1.31 and V1.32 there is no way to alter the used Texture during the game. Changing the Parent of a Highlight during the game can bug it, making it overglow sometimes.

FDF-Actions

  • HighlightAlphaFile
  • HighlightAlphaMode
  • HighlightColor
  • HighlightOutlineOffset
  • HighlightType
