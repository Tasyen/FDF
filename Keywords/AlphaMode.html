AlphaMode

Format: AlphaMode Text,

AlphaMode "ALPHAKEY",

AlphaMode "ADD",

AlphaMode "DISABLE",

AlphaMode "BLEND",

AlphaMode "MOD",

Purpose: Changes the way transparency of the taken image is interpreted, changing the image during game with BlzFrameSetTexture overwrittes this value.

Used in: Texture(SIMPLEFRAME)
